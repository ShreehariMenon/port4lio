Featured Project

STS Translate

A real-time speech-to-speech translator designed to address cross-language communication barriers in healthcare and education sectors. This comprehensive research project involved coordinating complex pipeline integration, combining Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), Machine Translation (MT), and Text-to-Speech (TTS) technologies into a seamless solution. The project required extensive validation with real-world scenarios to ensure practical applicability across diverse linguistic contexts. Working with healthcare and educational stakeholders, I designed and delivered a functional prototype that demonstrates significant potential for breaking down language barriers in critical communication scenarios.

Featured Project

Privacy-Preserving LLM Training with Federated Learning and Differential Privacy

A comprehensive research project conducted during my Summer Research Fellowship at IIT Bombay, focusing on developing secure and privacy-aware training methodologies for Large Language Models. This work explores the integration of Federated Learning and Differential Privacy techniques to enable distributed training while maintaining data confidentiality. Under PhD mentorship, I researched, built, and evaluated sophisticated LLM training pipelines that address critical challenges in scalable, domain-adaptive AI systems. The research addresses fundamental questions about balancing model accuracy with privacy preservation in real-world NLP applications, particularly relevant for sensitive domains like healthcare and finance.

Featured Project

Karna

A real-world healthcare application developed in collaboration with clinicians at Dr. S. R. Chandrasekhar Institute of Speech and Hearing. This project addresses critical challenges in audiological data collection and patient management through a user-friendly digital platform designed specifically for hearing disability assessments. Working directly with medical professionals, I defined comprehensive product requirements, created intuitive wireframes using Figma, and oversaw the complete development process to improve diagnostic workflows. The application bridges the gap between clinical expertise and modern technology, streamlining data collection processes while maintaining strict healthcare compliance standards.
